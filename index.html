My Poems

Cosmic Dance

Our minds blur at the edges
your thoughts, my thoughts, a single pulse.
Time, a living thread,
moving slowly through the strings, the universe hums.
A distant sun rises, black trees casting shadows on us, while we are drifting through the golden beams.

by Julie Webster

Unspoken


It's almost like I hear you saying, "So, what do you want?" "I want you," I said in my mind, but to you, I said, "Nothing."

And so you left,
thinking that this is what I meant to say.

by Julie Webster

Heartbreak

"Give it time," you said,
As if time was gentle,
As if it did not carve through me
like wind through withering branches.

by Julie Webster
